Frequently asked questions

What should I include in a sub plan PDF?

Typically your daily schedule and lesson notes, a class roster, a seating chart, emergency info, and any student handouts — all merged into one file for the day.

Can I reuse it next time?

Yes — keep the merged PDF and just swap in a new day plan next time you need a sub.

Is student data safe?

Yes. Rosters and seating charts contain student names, and ClassPDF processes everything in your browser — nothing is uploaded, so the data never leaves your device.
