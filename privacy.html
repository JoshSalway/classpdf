Privacy Policy

The short version: your files never leave your device. Every ClassPDF tool runs entirely in your web browser. When you open a PDF here, it is processed on your own computer - it is never uploaded to a server, and we never see it.

Your files

Nothing you open, edit, merge or convert is stored or transmitted. It lives only in your browser's memory while the page is open and is gone the moment you close the tab. That means a teacher's documents - and any student work in them - stay on the teacher's device.

Accounts

ClassPDF has no sign-up and no accounts. We don't ask for your name, email, or school details, and we don't have a login to store them in.

Analytics

We may use a privacy-respecting analytics tool to count anonymous page views so we know which tools are useful. It never receives your files and never identifies you personally.

Questions

ClassPDF is brought to you by the SproutSheets team. If anything here is unclear, reach out through the SproutSheets site.
