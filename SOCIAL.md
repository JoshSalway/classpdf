# Social Media Plan — ClassPDF

> Part of the fleet Social Playbook. This is ClassPDF's tailored plan.
> Core rule: **do not invent content, extract it** — the product itself is the content faucet.

**Tier:** 3 (Maintenance)  
**What it is:** Free, private, in-browser PDF tools for teachers: merge, split, compress.

## Audience
Teachers wrangling PDFs

## Where to post (ranked)
- Pinterest
- Facebook teacher groups
- Linked from ChalkBee (sister tool)

## Content pillars
- "Merge your worksheets into one PDF" how-tos
- The privacy angle (files never leave your device)
- Tool tips

## Cadence
2-3 pins/week; mostly ride ChalkBee's audience

## The wedge (why it spreads)
Do not run it standalone. Cross-promote from ChalkBee (identical audience). High-intent utility, low viral - let it ride the bigger sister site.

_Run everything through Postiz on a weekly batch. Make an asset once, cut it into a Pin, a Reel and a carousel._
